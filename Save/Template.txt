8 rows for pieces
each row will have 8 letter combinations
positive = white
negative = black
0 = empty
r = rook
h = knight
b = bishop
q = queen
k = king
p = pawn

after board state, number to indicate which player's turn it is
1 = white
0 = black

after player turn, two numbers to indicate mostRecentStartingSquare pair position

after mostRecentStartingSquare, two numbers to indicate mostRecentEndingSquare pair position

after mostRecentEndingSquare, letter combination to indicate piece stored in undoMovePieceStorage
positive = white
negative = black
0 = empty
r = rook
h = knight
b = bishop
q = queen
k = king
p = pawn
